# Gap Analysis Template

Use this after gathering initial sources. Track which data points are still missing.

## Format

| # | Data Point | Priority | Status | Source(s) | Notes |
|---|-----------|----------|--------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | Claim/fact | High/Med/Low | ✅ / ❌ / ⚠️ | URL, Date | What's still needed |

## Status Key
- ✅ **Verified** — source found, data confirmed
- ⚠️ **Partial** — source exists but incomplete (scrape failed, paywall, snippet only)
- ❌ **Missing** — no source yet, need to search

## Common Gaps in Indonesia Financial Research

- MSCI letter details (hard to find directly → search Kontan, Bisnis for "MSCI rebalancing")
- Rating agency full reports (Moody's, Fitch, S&P → Indonesian news coverage of their releases)
- Historical comparison data (TradingEconomics for macro, Kontan/Katadata for historical IHSG)
- Analyst quotes (IDXChannel, CNBC Indonesia for market commentary)
- Policy details/legal drafts (Kompas, Detik, Suara.com for policy angles)
- International context (Reuters, Bloomberg via Indonesian news re-publication)
- S&P/Moody's/Fitch direct statements vs Indonesian ministry re-interpretation
